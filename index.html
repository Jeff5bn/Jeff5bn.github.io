Work Experience

Data analyst for Energi Simulation Center for Geothermal Systems Research

During my time working over the summer of 2023, I got very familiar with geothermal drilling. I and a team of software engineering undergraduates were tasked with creating a web application that users can upload, view, and label various drilling data. We then had to apply a physics-based model for predicting downhole drilling attributes like rate of penetration and RPM using data from the surface. This model in combination with machine learning models would give the user reliable information on how a drilling of a well is going. This was in accordance with our industry partners to provide these tools that save time and resources while drilling. This software also has the goal of being eventually open-sourced and open for any individual to use in order to promote geothermal projects for a more sustainable heat and energy source. Progarmming Languanges used: Python, React, mySQL, fastapi (python backend)
